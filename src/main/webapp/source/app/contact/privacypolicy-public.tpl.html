Privacy Policy

The privacy, confidence, and trust of individuals who use the “Behavioral Health: Clinical Decisions Support” program are very important to us. No personal health information is collected at this site unless it is provided voluntarily by an individual while participating in an activity that asks for the information. We also collect statistical information that helps us understand how people are using the website so we can continually improve our services. The “Behavioral Health: Clinical Decisions Support” program is being used in a research study which has been approved by an accredited Institutional Review Board (IRB). Participant consent is being gathered. The IRB has approved the study and the data safety monitoring procedures associated with it. In the “Behavioral Health: Clinical Decisions Support” application, patient data elements are entered by the participant via a secured web browsing session and transferred to a secured database. When entering data, the browsing session is protected by SSL. This type of information is then transferred to an encrypted database, which is only accessible by authorized personnel. Communication between the web server and the database server is encrypted using SSL.

Compliance

Inflexxion is in material compliance with applicable privacy requirements because it (1) Has the approval of an accredited IRB to conduct this research study and its associated data safety procedures, and (2) Protects patient data elements received in “Behavioral Health: Clinical Decisions Support” by utilizing a secured web browsing session, and data encryption to protect its database.

Website Security

Inflexxion is committed to the security of the information that is either available from or collected by “Behavioral Health: Clinical Decisions Support”. Inflexxion has taken multiple steps to safeguard the integrity of its telecommunications and computing infrastructure, including but not limited to, authentication, monitoring, auditing, and encryption.

Email Disclaimer

Inflexxion’s Privacy Policy has no application to proprietary information, ideas or other intellectual property that you send to Inflexxion via email or otherwise. If you want to keep such information private or proprietary, then do not include them in an email to Inflexxion.
